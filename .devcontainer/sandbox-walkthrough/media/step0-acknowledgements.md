LLMoxie is developed by the
[University of Washington Scientific Software Engineering Center (UW SSEC)](https://escience.washington.edu/software-engineering/ssec/)
at the [eScience Institute](https://escience.washington.edu/).

This work is supported by the U.S. National Science Foundation (NSF) through
[NAIRR Pilot award #240292](https://nairrpilot.org/projects/awarded?_requestNumber=NAIRR240292),
and by [Schmidt Sciences](https://www.schmidtsciences.org/) through the
[Virtual Institutes for Scientific Software (VISS)](https://www.schmidtsciences.org/viss/) program.

Thank you to these organizations for making this sandbox possible — check the
step off and continue.
