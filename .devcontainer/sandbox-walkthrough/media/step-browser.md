## Use Google Chrome

This sandbox runs in the browser, and **Copilot Chat works best in
[Google Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/)**. Other Chromium-based
browsers usually work too, but Firefox and Safari can hit hiccups.

Click **Check browser** — you'll get a quick pass/fail:

- ✅ **Passed** — you're on a supported Chromium browser, move on.
- ⚠️ **Warning** — reopen this Codespace in Google Chrome for the smoothest
  experience.

> Working in the VS Code desktop app instead of a browser? The check can't see
> your browser from there, and that's fine — desktop is fully supported.
