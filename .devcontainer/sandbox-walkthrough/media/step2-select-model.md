## Pick a UW SSEC model

In Copilot Chat, click the **model name** next to the input box, expand
**Other Models**, and select `GPT 5.4 Mini (UW SSEC)` or
`GPT OSS 120B (UW SSEC)`.

![UW SSEC models under Other Models](llmoxie-model-picker-uw-ssec.png)

**Don't see them?** Click the gear icon in the picker once — the Language
Models manager makes the entries appear; close it and reopen the picker.

![Language Models manager with the LLMoxie Gateway section](llmoxie-language-models-manager.png)
