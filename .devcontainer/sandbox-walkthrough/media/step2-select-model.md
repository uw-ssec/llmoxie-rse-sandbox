## Pick a UW SSEC model

In Copilot Chat, click the model selector on the toolbar right underneath the
input box — it reads **Auto** by default. That opens the model picker.

The UW SSEC models don't appear here on their own. **Click the gear icon** at the
top of the picker to open the **Language Models** manager — this is the step that
surfaces them.

![Model picker — click the gear icon to open the Language Models manager](llmoxie-model-picker-default.png)

The UW SSEC models are listed at the **bottom** of the manager, under the
**LLMoxie Gateway** section: `GPT 5.5 (UW SSEC)` and `GPT 5.4 Mini (UW SSEC)`.
Close the manager once you've seen them there.

![Language Models manager — UW SSEC models under LLMoxie Gateway at the bottom](llmoxie-language-models-manager.png)

Now reopen the model selector and expand **Other Models** — the UW SSEC entries
are there. **Select `GPT 5.5 (UW SSEC)`**; it's the one to use for this sandbox.
The selector should then read `GPT 5.5 (UW SSEC)` instead of **Auto**.

![UW SSEC models under Other Models](llmoxie-model-picker-uw-ssec.png)
